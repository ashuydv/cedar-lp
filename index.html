Featured Artists

Talented performers coming to Navin's CEDAR Festive Night

Singer

Saindhavi

Saindhavi is one of the most refined voices in South Indian cinema, celebrated for her unique tone and unforgettable melodies. From soulful hits like Pirai Thedum, Yaaro Ivan, Kayile Aagasam, and Vizhigalil Oru Vaanavil to heartwarming lullabies, she brings magic every time she takes the stage.

Singer

Sridhar Sena

Sridhar Sena is one of the rising stars of South Indian music, known for his soulful voice and powerful stage presence. From delivering memorable songs in Tamil cinema to winning hearts in Super Singer, his performance of the Murugan song earned him special recognition and admiration across audiences.

Singer

Prasanna

Prasanna Adhisesha is an emerging voice in South Indian music, known for his journey in Super Singer and his soulful, energetic performances. With a style that connects instantly with the audience, he represents the fresh wave of talent in today's music scene.

Singer

Haripriya

Super Singer Haripriya is one of the standout voices from the show, known for her versatile singing and vibrant stage presence. With recent hits like Viral Vayyari and the energetic Bullet Song, her dazzling vocals have made her a rising sensation in South Indian music.
