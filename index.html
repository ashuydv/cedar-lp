About Navin's Cedar Tower

Navin's CEDAR, the grand finale of Starwood Towers in Medavakkam. It stands tall with Basement + Stilt + 21 floors, presenting 166 premium 3 BHK homes. Designed with a balcony for breathtaking views and a versatile multi-purpose room, every home is crafted for a lifestyle beyond the ordinary.

Spread across 9.85 acres of lush, landscaped spaces, CEDAR is surrounded by 100+ ready-to-use lifestyle amenities and a thriving community of 700+ happy families. This isn't just another phase, it's the crown jewel of Starwood Towers, where legacy meets modern living.

  • Singapore-style architectural elevation with modern design aesthetics
  • Premium location in Medavakkam with seamless city connectivity
  • Smart home features integrated throughout the apartments
  • High rental yield potential for investment opportunities
  • Comprehensive lifestyle amenities including an 18,500 sq.ft clubhouse
  • RERA registered project ensuring transparency and compliance
  • Expected possession by mid-2026 with quality construction timeline
